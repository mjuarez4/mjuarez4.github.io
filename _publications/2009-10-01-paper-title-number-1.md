This conference contribution presents **ActionPoint**, a parent–teen cyberbullying intervention app that combines brief coaching, conversation prompts, and guided response workflows. The app is designed to strengthen family communication and provide practical, evidence-informed steps when cyberbullying is suspected or reported.
